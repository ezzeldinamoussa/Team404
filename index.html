Accountability App

Functionality:

  • Set tasks and goals for the next day, a simple one-day-at-a-time planner that is gameified
  • For each task completed, you get a point. For 75% of all tasks done or higher for that day, your daily streak continues
  • Add friends who can see your points and streaks, view leadership board, etc. You and your friend can also complete a task together for double points
  • Rewards: With points you can buy profile badges like on discord, or other stuff, like a lifeline, allowing you to skip a task, or revive a broken streak for a higher cost, etc.
  • A restriction would be that the tasks you set for tomorrow cannot be removed after 11:59 today but can still add more tasks for today.
